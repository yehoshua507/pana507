Points to consider:

  • The page contains 4 buttons at the top to interact with the different types of information available.
  • Hovering your mouse over any point will show you an image of the associated point of interest.
  • The letter K stands for Kicks.
    • For example: 11 K = 11 kicks
  • Measurements depend on the individual diver and the type of fin used.
    • In my case, 1K = 58 inches or 4.83 feet.
